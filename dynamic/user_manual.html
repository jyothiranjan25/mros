Introduction

MROS system is a web based application that helps employees of concerned department during recruitment of new candidate to make the task less complicated and to be maintained digitally. Users can access the platform anytime, anywhere, and receive excellent work experience. The application scope will serve all the concerned employees of various departments required for manpower recruitment process.
The main scope is to cover all recruitment services such users can raise a request for offer letter, OLR approval by head of Academic Delivery Organization (ADO) / Service Organization (SO), while ensuring there is no breach in manpower headcount and budget by checking the budget and headcount, on-boarding the candidate, assigning the required resources, performing induction and finally separating the candidate from the system.

Abbreviations used.
Abbreviation Meaning
MROS Digital system for Manpower recruitment, on-boarding and separation
Headcount Total number of people to be recruited for the financial year
HR Head Human Resource Head
Candidate Prospect employee
OLR Offer Letter Request
CIF Candidate Information Form

Actors
# Actor Roles
1 Head HR Super Admin of the system
2 HR Access to task checklist, option to mark task as completed, and add comments for the completed tasks
3 Facility Owners Access to task checklist, option to mark task as completed, and add comments for the completed tasks. User and role management
4 Head View/Edit/ Approve - Offer Letter
Access report to:
Download Headcount & Budget Report for the respective ADO/SO
Download all offer and appointment letter for the ADO/SO
5 Coordinator Raise request for Offer letter (OLR)
6 Finance Controller Access any modifications required in budget or headcount
6 New Employee Accept Offer Letter send in their personal mail

Role of Super Admin

Super Admin in MROS system will handle events like :

  • Adding Entity
  • Adding Department
  • Adding/Editing Roles
  • Adding Position
  • Adding Job Title
  • Adding/Editing Masters
  • Adding/Editing Offer Letter Template

Adding Entity

To add new entity, please follow the steps detailed below.

  • STEP 1 : Enter name of Entity in input text field

  • STEP 2 : Next click on Submit

  • STEP 3 : Message will pop up stating that a new enity has been added.

Adding Department

To add new Departent, please follow the steps detailed below.

  • STEP 1 : Enter name of Departent in input text field

  • STEP 2 : Next click on "Add More" Button (if you want to add more than one department at a time)

  • STEP 3 : Next click on Submit

  • STEP 4 : Message will pop up stating that a new department has been added.

  • Notice : Departent are added on the basis of the entity.

Adding/Editing Roles

To add new role, please follow the steps detailed below.

  • STEP 1:Enter Role name in input text field

  • STEP 2:Choose any permission given to that role from the checkbox listed.Each Permission will have different access to the system

    • "GENERATE OFFER LETTER" -> This permission is basically for Coordinator who will have accessibility to apply for offer letter
    • "Accept/Reject Offer Letter Request" -> This permission is basically for ADO/SO Head who will have accessibility to Accept/Reject offer letters
    • "Approve Offer Letter Request" -> This permission is basically for ADO/SO Head who will have accessibility to Approve offer letters
    • "Sent Offer Letter Request To Candidate" -> This permission is basically for HR who will have accessibility to Send the offer letters to the candidate. He/She will also have accessibility to view Offer Letter before sending and can also view the CIF details updated by the candidate.
    • "View Offer Letter" -> This permission will have accessibility to view offer letters to the candidate. He/She will also have accessibility to view Offer Letter.
    • "Accounts" -> This permission is basically for Finance Controller who will have accessibility to update budget and headcount.
    • "Manage Assets" -> This permission is basically for Facility Owners who will have accessibility to make sure that all the entitlments has been provided to new employee during onboarding and has been recieved from employee during seperation .
    • "Super Admin" -> This permission is basically for HR Head who will have accessibility to add entity,department,roles,position,job title,currency,Masters .
    • "New Employee" -> This permission is basically for New Employee who will have accessibility to confirm the entitlments recieved to them as well as will be able to fill the feedback form.They will also be able to fill the exit interview form during the process of separation.
    • "IT" -> This permission is basically for IT who will have accessibility to make employee id as well as employee mail id which will be sent to them from HR.

  • STEP 3:Once the checklist and name has been confirmed , click on "Submit" button

  • STEP 4:Message will pop up stating that a new role has been added.

Adding Position

To add new Position, please follow the steps detailed below.

  • STEP 1:Enter name of Position in input text field

  • STEP 2:Next click on Submit

  • STEP 3:Message will pop up stating that a new position has been added.

Adding Job Title

To add new Job Title, please follow the steps detailed below.

  • STEP 1 : Enter name of Job Title in input text field

  • STEP 2 : Next click on "Add More" Button (if you want to add more than one Job Title at a time)

  • STEP 3 : Next click on Submit

  • STEP 4 : Message will pop up stating that a new Job Title has been added.

  • Notice : Job Title are added on the basis of the position added previously.

Adding/Editing Masters

To add new Masters, please follow the steps detailed below.

  • STEP 1 : Select the mail id from the dropdown.The list shows all employees mail id

  • STEP 2 : Enter the name of the Master( this name will be shown after Welcome in the left side of the dashboard )

  • STEP 3 : Select the role from the dropdown. These role will give that particular employee the accessibility of that particular role in this system.

  • STEP 4 : Select the department from the dropdown menu for that employee

  • STEP 5 : Click on "Submit" button.

  • Notice : Masters are added on the basis of the entity.

Adding/Editing Offer Letter Template

To add new Offer Letter Template, please follow the steps detailed below.

  • STEP 1 : Select the Enity name for which the template needs to be created.

  • STEP 2 : Write the Offer Letter Template name in the input text field

  • STEP 3 : Enter the template details (or the template content ) that will be shown to the candidate when Offer Letter will be send.

  • STEP 4 : Click on "Submit" button.

  • Notice : The template content should carry variables listed at the bottom .These variables can be used anywhere in the template whenever required .Each variable have small description about how it will be recorded into the template.
